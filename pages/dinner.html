Sri Lankan Pol Roti

Pol Roti is a traditional Sri Lankan flatbread that carries the essence of rustic island cooking. Made with a combination of grated coconut, flour, salt, and a dash of water, this humble bread is not only rich in flavor but also deeply rooted in Sri Lankan culinary heritage. The grated coconut is the key ingredient, infusing the bread with a subtle sweetness and a delightful texture that is both chewy and soft on the inside, while the outer crust achieves a satisfying crispiness when cooked on a hot griddle. The dough is typically kneaded until smooth, then shaped into rounds and cooked on a tava or flat pan, resulting in golden-brown, slightly puffed-up flatbreads that are aromatic and comforting.
